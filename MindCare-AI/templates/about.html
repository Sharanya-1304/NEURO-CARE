About MindCare AI

Our Mission

MindCare AI is dedicated to making mental health assessment accessible to everyone. We believe that early detection and intervention can make a significant difference in people's lives. Our AI-powered system provides quick, accurate assessments to help individuals understand their stress levels and depression risk.

How It Works

Our system uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on real-world data to evaluate multiple factors including sleep patterns, work pressure, anxiety levels, social interaction, and lifestyle habits. The Random Forest classifier provides accurate predictions while maintaining high interpretability.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and processed locally on your device. We do not store, share, or sell any personal information. Your assessment results are yours alone.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python, Flask
  • Machine Learning: Scikit-learn, Random Forest
  • Data Analysis: Pandas, NumPy
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Visualization: Plotly, Matplotlib

Disclaimer

Important: MindCare AI is a supportive tool designed to help with self-assessment. It is NOT a substitute for professional medical or psychological advice. If you are experiencing severe mental health issues, please contact a qualified healthcare professional or emergency services immediately.

Accuracy & Reliability

Our models are trained on large, diverse datasets and regularly evaluated for accuracy and reliability. However, mental health assessment is complex, and this tool should be used as one of many resources for understanding your mental well-being.

Support Resources

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-8255

Development

MindCare AI is an open-source project developed to promote mental health awareness and provide accessible mental health assessment tools. The system is continuously improved based on user feedback and latest research in mental health and artificial intelligence.
