📊 Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard

📌 Project Overview
The Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, customer behavior, and product category insights. This dashboard helps organizations make data-driven decisions by providing clear visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs).

---

🎯 Objectives
- Monitor overall sales and revenue performance.
- Analyze sales across different product categories.
- Track monthly sales trends.
- Identify top-performing categories and customers.
- Compare sales performance across regions.
- Provide actionable business insights through data visualization.

---

📈 Dashboard Features

1. KPI Cards
- Total Revenue
- Total Quantity Sold
- Customer Count

 2. Sales by Category
- Revenue generated by each product category:
  - Beauty
  - Books
  - Electronics
  - Fashion
  - Home & Kitchen
  - Sports

 3. Profit by Year
- Year-wise profit comparison.
- Helps identify business growth trends.

 4. Top Customers Profit Analysis
- Displays top customers contributing the highest profit.

 5. Sales by Region
- Regional sales comparison:
  - Middle East
  - North America
  - Asia
  - Europe

 6. Monthly Sales Analysis
- Month-wise sales performance.
- Helps identify seasonal trends.

 7. Interactive Filters
- Product Category Filter
- Month Filter
- Year Filter
- Quarter Filter
